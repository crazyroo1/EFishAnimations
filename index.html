Hello!

My name is Ella Fisher, I'm a sophomore at Kennesaw State University currently working towards a Bachelor's degree in Digital Animation. I am currently in KSU's Honors College with a 4.0 GPA.

I've been drawing for as long as I can remember, and animating my drawings since high school. I'm most familiar with 2D frame-by-frame animation using Toon Boom Harmony, and will be learning other animation forms within the next few years. Character design is another passion of mine. In my free time, I love doodling new fantastical creatures. I also love to create storyboards, as I find storyboarding is the best way to visualize my ideas quickly.

Check out the Animations tab for some examples of my work, and the Art tab for some more realistic works. Also be sure to take a peek of my Instagram for some original characters and simple art!
